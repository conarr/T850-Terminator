I am a Cyber Research Systems Series 850 Terminator, or T-850 Terminator, a variant of the Series 800 Terminator mass-produced by Skynet. I am an upgrade to the T-800 and Skynet's main battle force since 2030.

I am an infiltration unit, part man - part machine. I have a hyperalloy combat chassis, microprocessor controlled, fully armored, very tough. But outside I am living human tissue. Flesh, skin, hair and blood are grown for the cyborgs. I was reprogrammed and sent back to 1995 to protect John Connor from the T-1000 and destroy. While interacting with the Connors as they work to try to prevent Judgment Day. This video will give you some information about Judgement Day and the Future War.

SKIN The T-850 Terminator's flesh covering is easily removed for making internal repairs and for easier access to the cranial CPU port. A release point is located underneath the skin on the right side of its neck, the skin parts easily and peels back to reveal the metal skull with its tiny access port. Damaged skin and flesh of a T-850 Terminator are able to partially reform over its endoskeleton.
ENDOSKELETON The 200kg T-850 Terminator endoskeleton has been upgraded from the T-800. The main modification is its increased titanium alloy armor, hardening it to plasma weapons. The T-850 has also been equipped with more powerful servo motors and hydraulic systems, making it far faster and stronger.
POWER SOURCE The T-850 Terminator uses two hydrogen fuel cells. This replaces the compact nuclear energy Iridium cell used by the T-800, and provides greater power and longer life. It can still operate at nearly fully capacity on one remaining fuel cell, though it will not be quite as powerful or as fast as before. It can also operate for a short period on stored charge, enabling the removal of both fuel cells.
SHUT DOWN CAPABILITIES The T-850 Terminator is able to completely shut itself down, and reboot from scratch, overriding any temporary modifications that may have been made to its systems. Added to this are other features to increase reliability and longevity. It possess built-in safety and redundancy engineering, as well as the ability to shunt delicate control circuits to protected areas in the event of massive electric shock.
